ABOUT THE ARTIST

The Sound of Tomorrow

DJ Metro is an electronic music producer and DJ known for his unique blend of house, techno, and future bass. With over a decade of experience in the industry, Metro has developed a signature sound that's both innovative and deeply rooted in dance music traditions.

Musical Journey

From underground clubs to major festivals, Metro has shared the stage with industry giants while staying true to his underground roots.

Production Style

Metro's productions are characterized by deep basslines, intricate percussion, and emotive melodies that create an immersive listening experience.
